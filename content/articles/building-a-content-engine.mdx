## One Idea Becomes Many Assets

For a beginner creator, the easiest system is repurposing. One useful idea can become:

- One short TikTok video
- One carousel
- One blog note
- One newsletter paragraph
- One product comparison post

## The Low-Cost Stack

Start with free or cheap tools:

- Notion or Google Docs for ideas
- CapCut for short video editing
- Canva free plan for thumbnails
- Google Sheets for affiliate tracking
- This blog for long-form trust building

## Measure What Matters

Track views, clicks, saves, comments, and conversion rate. A small creator can improve quickly by reviewing one simple sheet every week.
